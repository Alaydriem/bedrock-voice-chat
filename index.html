Why Bedrock Voice Chat?

Built for Gamers

Proximity Chat

Positional spatial audio, with configurable voice ranges and falloff curves for realistic in-game communication, whisper modes, and spectator mode support

Encrypted

All voice data is fully encrypted. Your conversations stay private and secure.

Group Chats

Create persistent audio channels for teams, parties, or server-wide communication.

Audio Controls

Mute, deafen, adjust individual player volume from 0-150%, noise suppression, and voice amplification.

Audio Recording

Record voice sessions to separate tracks per player with timecode support for content creation.

Cross-Platform

Available on Windows and Android. macOS and iOS coming soon. Console players can use the mobile app.

Cross-Game

Works with Minecraft Bedrock Edition, Java Edition (Fabric & Paper), and Hytale.

Smart Voice Detection

Automatic voice activation or Push-to-Talk. Supports ASIO and WASAPI audio devices.

WebSocket API

Automate and integrate with Stream Deck, custom plugins, and other automation tools.
